Privacy Policy

At Compassionate Care Advisors LLC, your privacy is our top priority. Updated October 24, 2025

We are committed to maintaining transparency about how we handle your data. This includes information collected through online forms, cookies, and communications to enhance your experience while ensuring compliance with HIPAA and applicable data protection laws.

Overview

This policy applies to all data collected through our website compassionatecareadvisors.com, email communications, and other digital platforms operated by Compassionate Care Advisors LLC. It describes the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the safeguards we have in place to protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information to improve our services and provide you with a personalized experience. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any information you provide through forms or communication.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior on our website.
  • Cookies: Used to improve user experience and analyze website performance.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve our services.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send updates, newsletters, or promotional content (only with your consent).
  • Analyze website usage and enhance functionality.

Security and Retention

We implement advanced security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All sensitive data transmitted between you and our website is encrypted using SSL technology. We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties unless required by law or necessary to deliver services (e.g., trusted vendors under strict confidentiality agreements).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, where applicable.
